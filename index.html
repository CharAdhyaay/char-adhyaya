About Us

Char Adhyaya is a non-profit organization committed to improving the lives of children, youth, and the elderly through impactful initiatives in health, education, livelihood, and climate resilience.

Our Causes

  • Health and Nutrition: Supporting underprivileged children with essential healthcare.
  • Education: Providing learning opportunities for underprivileged youth.
  • Livelihood: Empowering families through skill development and resources.
  • Climate Change: Promoting awareness and resilience against climate challenges.
  • Humanitarian Responses: Offering emergency aid during crises.
